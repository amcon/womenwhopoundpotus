From the mind of Erica Vlahinos, WOMEN WHO POUND POTUS is the politically and gossip fueled comedy that spills the true tales of the real women who drove our Presidents really wild. Whether it was 69 in the situation room, whips in the West Wing or a romp in the rose garden, the broads with the biggest influence were the blackmailing female athletes, models, actresses and scandal causing staff, and these ladies never acted First Lady-Like. In WOMEN WHO POUND POTUS, a certain former intern receives an invitation to get back in action twenty years after first getting rug-burn in the oval office. As she ponders pounding an orange POTUS, she gets guidance from the spirits of the women who stood (and lied down) in her shoes.
